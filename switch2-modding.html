⚠️ MIG Flash Cartridge – Current Limitations
• Supports backup loading of Nintendo Switch 1 titles only when used on Switch 2 (backwards-compatible mode).
• No known way to run Switch 2 native backups or commercial dumps. High telemetry & ban risk if used online.
• Firmware future updates might add Switch 2 support, but security chips remain a barrier.
🛡️ Why so slow compared to Switch 1?
Nintendo doubled down on bootROM protections, introduced hardware-based key derivation, and patched known Tegra exploits. The modding scene is rebuilding tools from scratch. Expect months or possibly 1-2 years for homebrew entrypoints.
