About BazMartin

I'm BazMartin, also known as Barry. I'm an experienced roleplayer within SAMP and MTA Communities, alongside being a member of the Switch Modding Community. This is my hub of resources and tools that I have created, to make life easier for folks across SAMP/MTA and Switch Modding.

Regular updates happen here, with things getting improved and tweaked. All of the code is available publically via Github, and can be used for your own projects. Happy to help via Discord with code I've produced too.

📌 Why this hub exists

I often have ideas to either help myself, or people in the communities listed. Instead of local hosting things to improve my own experience, might as well publically share them, providing a quicker way of doing things, or an alternative solution to others too.
